Automatic login to Vodafone-WiFi hotspots in Italy.

This script is cross-platform and can be used on Linux, Windows, and macOS.
It allows automatic connection to captive portals.

The script and documentation are available on
[GitHub](https://github.com/blackwiz4rd/Vodafone-WiFi-AutoLogin).
