  A machine learning–based solution for selecting tile rates in 360° video streaming to head-mounted displays.

During a 360° video streaming session, video segments can be divided into tiles.  
Selecting the appropriate tiles at the correct quality reduces bandwidth consumption while ensuring a high quality of experience.
